1、wget https://mirrors.tuna.tsinghua.edu.cn/apache/zookeeper/zookeeper-3.4.13/zookeeper-3.4.13.tar.gz

2、tar -zxvf zookeeper-3.4.13.tar.gz

3、将conf文件夹下zoo_sample.cfg复制一份，改名为zoo.cfg
      cd zookeeper-3.4.13/conf/
      cp zoo_sample.cfg zoo.cfg

4、创建data目录
mkdir zookeeper-3.4.13/data
   
5、vi zoo.cfg
dataDir=/usr/local/wujk/zookeeper-3.4.13/data

6、启动zookeeper：bin/zkServer.sh start
关闭zookeeper：bin/zkServer.sh stop
查看zookeeper状态：bin/zkServer.sh status
